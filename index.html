Everything You Need to Listen Together

📶

WiFi-Only Sharing

No internet needed. Connect directly with friends over your local WiFi network using peer-to-peer technology. Fast, private, and reliable.

🎵

Any Music Source

Import your own MP3s, search Apple Music, or browse Spotify. B2Gaudio works with the music you already have and love.

👥

Synchronized Listening

One person hosts a session, others join and hear the same music at the same time. Like a silent disco with your friends.

📋

Create Mixtapes

Build playlists from any source and share them with your group. Drag and drop to reorder, shuffle for variety.

🔀

Full Player Controls

Play, pause, skip, rewind, shuffle, and reorder your queue. All the controls you expect from a modern music player.

🔒

Private & Secure

Your music stays on your network. No data sent to servers, no tracking, no accounts required. Just you and your friends.

How It Works

1

Import or Connect Your Music

Add local audio files from your device, connect Apple Music, or link your Spotify account. All your music in one place.

2

Host a Listening Session

Tap "Start Session" to create a room. Your phone becomes the host, broadcasting to anyone on the same WiFi network.

3

Friends Join In

Nearby friends open B2Gaudio and see your session. One tap to join, and they're listening along in perfect sync.

4

Enjoy Together

Build the queue together, share mixtapes, and enjoy the music. It's that simple.
