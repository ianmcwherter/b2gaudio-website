Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share music with friends?

Make sure all devices are on the same WiFi network. Open B2Gaudio and tap "Start Session" to host. Your friends open the app and will see your session appear — they just tap to join. That's it!

Do I need an internet connection?

No! B2Gaudio works entirely over your local WiFi network using peer-to-peer connections. No internet is required for sharing local files. However, if you want to search Apple Music or Spotify, you'll need internet for those searches.

How do I add music to the app?

There are three ways: (1) Import local files (MP3, M4A, WAV, etc.) by tapping the + button in the Library tab. (2) Search Apple Music from the search tab in Library. (3) Search Spotify from the search tab in Library. You can connect Apple Music and Spotify from the Settings tab.

How do I connect Apple Music?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Apple Music" under Streaming Services. The app will ask for permission to access Apple Music. Once granted, you can search and play songs from the Apple Music catalog. You need an active Apple Music subscription.

How do I connect Spotify?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Spotify" under Streaming Services. You'll be redirected to Spotify's website to log in and authorize B2Gaudio. Once connected, you can search Spotify from the Library. You need a Spotify Premium account for full playback control.

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings and tap the "Remove Ads" button under the Premium section. It's a one-time purchase of $0.99 that permanently removes all banner ads from the app. If you've already purchased, tap "Restore Purchases."

Why can't my friend see my session?

Make sure both devices are connected to the same WiFi network. Also check that "Local Network" permissions are enabled for B2Gaudio in your iPhone's Settings app (Settings > Privacy & Security > Local Network). Try restarting the session if it still doesn't appear.

What audio formats are supported?

B2Gaudio supports MP3, M4A, WAV, AIFF, AAC, FLAC, and OGG audio files. For video, it supports MP4, MOV, AVI, and MKV files.

How do I reorder songs?

In the Library, tap "Reorder" in the top left. You can then drag and drop songs to rearrange them. The same works in Mixtapes and in the playback queue (tap the queue button in the Now Playing screen).

Is my data private?

Yes. B2Gaudio doesn't collect any personal data or send anything to external servers. All your music, playlists, and preferences stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

System Requirements

B2Gaudio requires iOS 16.0 or later and works on iPhone. A WiFi connection is needed for sharing music between devices.
